⚽ World Cup 2026 Tracker

Flash the firmware to your Waveshare ESP32-S3-Touch-LCD-1.28 — right from this page.

  1. Use Chrome or Edge on a desktop (they support Web Serial).
  2. Plug the board into your computer with a USB-C cable.
  3. Click Connect below and choose the serial port.
  4. Click Install and wait — it takes about a minute.
Your browser doesn't support Web Serial — please use desktop Chrome or Edge. This page must be served over HTTPS to flash.
After flashing: the tracker creates a Wi-Fi hotspot called WorldCup-Setup. Join it, then enter your Wi-Fi and a free football-data.org token. Full instructions are in the README.

Your Wi-Fi password and API token are stored only on the device and are never part of this firmware.
